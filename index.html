About Me

Friendly, fast-learning, and motivated professional seeking a barista position in Seattle. Passionate about coffee, hospitality, and creating a welcoming experience for every guest.
Completed hands-on Barista Training Program at Evergreen Goodwill (July 2025), where I gained skills in espresso preparation, drink consistency, customer interaction, and café operations. I take pride in being dependable, attentive to detail, and a positive team player.
I’m excited to bring my enthusiasm for coffee culture, strong communication skills, and ability to thrive in fast-paced environments to a café team that values quality, connection, and care.
